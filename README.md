Project Overview

This project analyzes a Netflix movie dataset to understand movie popularity, ratings, genres, languages, and release trends. The analysis was performed using Python for data cleaning, statistical analysis, and data visualization.

Project Objectives

. Analyze movie popularity and ratings

. Study genre distribution

. Explore language preferences

. Identify relationships between popularity, votes, and ratings

. Discover trends in movie releases over time

Key Insights

Movie Popularity

. Movie popularity is highly skewed.

. Most movies have low to moderate popularity scores.

. A small number of blockbuster movies have extremely high popularity.

. This shows that only a few movies attract the most audience attention.

Movie Ratings

. Most movies have ratings between 6 and 8.

. Very few movies have extremely low or extremely high ratings.

. This indicates that most movies receive average to good audience feedback.

Vote Count

. Most movies receive relatively few votes.

. A small number of movies receive very large numbers of votes.

. This suggests audience engagement is concentrated among a limited number of titles.

Genre Distribution

. Drama is one of the most common genres.

. Action and Comedy also represent a significant share of movies.

. Some genres appear much less frequently.

. This shows that Netflix offers a wide variety of content while focusing heavily on popular genres.

Language Distribution

. English is the dominant language in the dataset.

. Other languages contribute a smaller portion of the catalog.

. This indicates that English-language content makes up the majority of available movies.

Correlation Analysis

. Popularity and vote count show a weak positive relationship.

. Vote count and ratings show a moderate positive relationship.

. Popularity and ratings have very little correlation.

. This means popular movies are not always the highest-rated movies.

Release Trends

. Movie releases increased over time.

. Recent years contain a larger number of movies compared to earlier years.

. This suggests continuous growth in movie production and content availability.

Recommendations

. Promote High-Rated Movies

. Highlight highly rated movies to improve user engagement and viewing satisfaction.

. Improve Visibility of Lesser-Known Titles

. Use recommendation systems to increase exposure for quality movies with lower popularity.

. Expand Diverse Genre Content.

. Continue investing in multiple genres to attract different audience preferences.

. Increase International Content.

. Expand content in different languages to reach broader global audiences.

. Use ratings and vote counts to identify successful content and guide future investments.

Conclusion

The analysis shows that movie popularity, ratings, genres, and audience engagement vary significantly across the Netflix catalog. While a small number of movies dominate popularity and votes, most movies receive moderate ratings and engagement. Genre diversity and increasing movie releases demonstrate Netflix's broad content strategy. Understanding these patterns can help improve recommendations, audience satisfaction, and content planning.
